Project Overview

SoulBuddy is a mental wellness web application designed to provide users with AI-driven emotional
support and self-care resources. The platform aims to offer a safe and empathetic space for
individuals seeking mental health assistance, leveraging technology to make support more accessible.

The application features an intuitive interface where users can engage in conversations with an AI
companion trained to recognize and respond to various emotional states. Additionally, SoulBuddy
offers curated self-help content, including mindfulness exercises and coping strategies, to assist
users in managing stress and improving their mental well-being.

The development of SoulBuddy focused on creating a responsive and user-friendly experience. By
utilizing Streamlit, the team was able to rapidly prototype and deploy the application, ensuring
seamless interaction across devices. The integration of AI technologies enables the app to provide
personalized support, making mental health resources more engaging and effective.
